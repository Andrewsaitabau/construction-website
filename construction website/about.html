About PrimeBuild Construction

Our Story

Founded in 2003, PrimeBuild Construction has grown from a small local contractor to one of the region's most trusted construction firms. What began as a family-owned business with just a handful of employees has blossomed into a full-service construction company with over 150 skilled professionals.

Our Mission

We are committed to building structures of exceptional quality while providing an outstanding client experience. Our mission is to deliver projects that stand the test of time through craftsmanship, integrity, and innovative solutions.

Our Values

At PrimeBuild, we operate with core values that guide every project: Quality, Safety, Integrity, Innovation, and Client Satisfaction. These principles are the foundation of our company culture and the reason our clients continue to choose us year after year.

Our Commitment to Sustainability

At PrimeBuild Construction, sustainability is more than a trend — it’s a responsibility. We utilize green building practices, energy-efficient materials, and waste-reduction techniques to ensure every project contributes positively to the environment. We proudly follow LEED certification principles and work with local suppliers to reduce our carbon footprint.
